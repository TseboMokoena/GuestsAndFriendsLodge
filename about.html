About us

Success Story

We find ourselves travelling to various family gatherings such as weddings, funerals and parties. This leads to extensive travel locally and abroad since we have families who live far from us.

We love to be comfortable and to rest after our long travels, as a result, we always check into hotels or guesthouses. This also prevents us from bothering our family members with accommodation or sleeping in cramped and overcrowded rooms.

Then these questions came to mind, what if other people have similar challenges? What if people locally would like to have their friends and families stay as close to them as possible, including walking to a guesthouse? That's when the idea of a high-quality guesthouse right here in the township came about. Guests and Friends Lodge was the answer.

Guests and Friends Lodge opened its doors on the 1st September 2017. We accommodated two guests on the opening day. Hooraay!
